About ERACE

ERACE is the premier student association of the Department of Civil Engineering at PET Engineering College. Our name is an acronym for "Empowering Rebuilding And Construction Excellence", reflecting our commitment to the fundamental principles of the civil engineering profession.

Since its inception, ERACE has served as a bridge between classroom learning and field application. We empower students to take ownership of their professional development by organizing national-level technical symposia, workshops, and peer-mentoring sessions that go beyond the university syllabus.
