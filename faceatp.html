Official Charter

FACEAT&P

Founded at PETEC in 2016

The FACEAT&P student Chapter of PETEC was established in 2016 at PET Engineering College by the Department of Civil Engineering. It provides students with unparalleled opportunities to learn directly from industry experts, gain real-world experience, and build professional relationships with potential employers.

The chapter organizes a variety of events throughout the year, including seminars, workshops, technical competitions, and site visits. These initiatives allow students to stay abreast of the latest trends and technologies in the construction industry while networking with licensed practitioners.
