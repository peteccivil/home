Innovation & Precision

Bridging Academic Excellence with Industrial Precision

Bvisor is a civil engineering startup established by the Department of Civil Engineering, PET Engineering College, providing end-to-end engineering design and consultancy services.

We specialise in structural design, soil investigation, architectural drafting, BIM modelling, and sustainable environmental solutions. Our unique industry–academia collaboration ensures technical excellence, accuracy, and client-focused service delivery. From Tamil Nadu to Dubai, our work reflects quality, innovation, and trust.

Engineering Services

Professional Solutions for Residential, Commercial & Industrial Projects

Structural Design

Precision Engineering

Structural consultancy for villas and industrial sheds using seismic and wind load analysis (IS 456 / IS 800) to optimize material costs.

BIM & Revit

Visualize Before You Build

Building Information Modelling (BIM) for digital twins and clash detection between structural and MEP elements before site work.

Soil Testing

Solid Foundations

Advanced onsite sampling and laboratory testing for Soil Bearing Capacity (SBC) and detailed geotechnical reporting.

Architectural Planning

AutoCAD Drafting

Detailed floor plans, structural layouts, and modern elevation design with high-quality 3D rendering for façade development.

MEP & Water Supply

Integrated Systems

Complete design for HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and fire systems. Municipal and urban water supply planning.

Estimation & BOQ

Zero Surprises

Detailed Bills of Quantities (BOQ) and material take-offs to prevent cost overruns during construction.

The Bvisor Story

Academic Roots, Industrial Standards

Bvisor is a civil engineering startup launched as an initiative to provide high-quality, real-world engineering solutions while creating meaningful learning opportunities for young engineers.

"Built on a strong industry–academia collaboration model, Bvisor operates in partnership with our technical collaborator Al Mussiver Structural Consultants, ensuring every project meets professional standards and practical execution requirements."
