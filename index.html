MEET BVISOR

Bvisor is a civil engineering startup by the Department of Civil Engineering, PET Engineering College. We bridge the gap between academia and industry, delivering professional-grade solutions for global infrastructure projects.

Operating in partnership with Al Mussiver Structural Consultants, we combine faculty expertise and modern tools to ensure excellence and real-world training for young engineers.

Student Excellence & Leadership

E

Erace

Erace is our premier Department Association, serving as the heartbeat of student activities. It provides a platform for technical symposia, talent showcases, and peer-to-peer knowledge sharing, fostering a vibrant community of future engineers.

View Association Activities
F

FACEAT&P Chapter

We host a prestigious student chapter of the Federation of All Civil Engineer Associations of Tamilnadu & Puducherry (FACEAT&P). Since 2016, we have been among the first in the region to host this network, providing students direct access to professional engineering bodies.
