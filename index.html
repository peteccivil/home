About US

The Department of Civil Engineering was established in the year 2012 and offers an under graduate four year degree programme in B.E. The Department trains the students in such a way to meet the demands of the job market in the construction industries.

Our Mission

  • 01

    To provide students with a broad and thorough education in civil engineering fundamentals.

  • 02

    To provide a holistic education for the students with conceptual and practical insight.

Our Vision

"To be a centre of excellence in civil engineering and contribute to environmental preservation and overall sustainable development."
